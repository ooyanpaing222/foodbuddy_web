Privacy Policy for Food Buddy

Effective Date: 07-Nov-24

Introduction

Food Buddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our AI-powered chatbot application.

Information We Collect

We do not store or retain any personal data or conversation content. When you interact with the chatbot, your input is sent to AI models to generate a relevant response. This process is performed solely to provide real-time answers to your queries, and we do not retain or save any data on our servers.

Data Usage

Your input is only processed to provide you with accurate and helpful responses. We do not store, share, or sell your data.

Third-Party Processors

We rely on third-party AI model providers to generate responses based on your input. These providers may process your data momentarily during the response generation. Each provider complies with relevant data protection regulations and operates under strict confidentiality requirements.

Security

We take appropriate measures to safeguard your information during transmission. However, please be aware that while we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@imbuddy.site.
